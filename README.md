# 🎀 Cinnamoroll Mansion

A cute fan-made browser game for kids 6–12. Create your own character (girl or
boy — pick skin, hair, outfit and accessories), then explore the mansion and
play mini-games with all the Hello Kitty friends!

## How to play

Just open `index.html` in any modern browser (double-click it), or serve the
folder for best results:

```sh
python3 -m http.server 8000
# then visit http://localhost:8000
```

No installs, no build step — everything is plain HTML5 canvas + JavaScript.

### On a tablet 📱

The game is tablet-ready in any mobile browser. Hold the tablet **sideways
(landscape)** — if you're in portrait it'll show a friendly "turn me sideways"
screen. Everything scales to fit the screen, with no accidental zooming or
scrolling, and it respects rounded corners / notches. Tap the **fullscreen
button** (top right) to hide the browser bars, or "Add to Home Screen" to play
it like a real app.

## Controls

| Action | Mouse | Keyboard | Touch |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Walk | click where to go | WASD / Arrow keys | drag on the left side, or tap where to go |
| Talk / confirm | click a friend / button | Space / Enter / E | tap a friend, or the ★ button |
| Quit a mini-game | Escape | ✕ button (top right) |
| Mute / unmute | M | speaker button (top right) |
| Fullscreen | — | ⛶ button (top right, in the mansion) |

## The games

- 🎳 **Bowling** with Hello Kitty
- ⚾ **Baseball** with Pompompurin
- 🌼 **Hide & Seek** in the garden with Cinnamoroll
- 🎴 **Memory Match** with My Melody
- 🐟 **Fishing Pond** with Keroppi
- 🎵 **Dance Party** with Kuromi
- 🚦 **Red Light, Green Light** with Badtz-Maru — walk on green, freeze on red!
  (talk to him on the sofa)
- ⭐ **Coin Hunt** with Pochacco — run around the room grabbing goodies before
  the timer (ask him at the shop)

The last two are walk-around games you start by talking to the friends hanging
out in the mansion.

### 🌳 The Backyard

Head out the **patio door** (bottom-left of the mansion) to the backyard, where
all the same friends are hanging out for some outdoor games:

- ⛳ **Putt-Putt** mini-golf with Pompompurin — pull back and putt!
- 🎵 **Follow the Leader** with Cinnamoroll — copy the moves!
- 🔢 **Hopscotch** with Hello Kitty — hop the squares in order
- ⚽ **Penalty Kicks** with Pochacco — aim past Keroppi the goalie
- 💦 **Water Balloon Toss** with Badtz-Maru — splash the targets
- 🪢 **Jump Rope** with Kuromi — time your jumps!
- 🚦 **Red Light, Green Light** — Keroppi points you to Badtz-Maru's game
- 🦋 **Butterfly Catch** with My Melody — run around with your net

Every friend hosts an outdoor game now! Walk up to a friend to play. Tap **🏠
Inside** at the top to go back into the mansion, or **🏊 Pool** at the bottom to
head to the pool. Earn coins in every game — your coins and best scores are saved
automatically in your browser.

## The Pool 🏊

Through the pool gate at the bottom of the backyard is the swimming pool, with six
splashy water games — all the same friends are here too:

- 🌊 **Cannonball Splash** with Keroppi — time your bounce for the biggest splash
- 🏊 **Pool Race** with Pochacco — tap fast to swim and beat Badtz-Maru
- 🛝 **Water Slide** with Cinnamoroll — steer down the slide and grab the rings
- 🏐 **Beach Ball** with Pompompurin — keep the ball bouncing
- 🦆 **Duck Round-Up** with My Melody — tap the rubber duckies before they drift off
- 🔫 **Squirt Splash** with Kuromi — squirt the targets that pop up (whack-a-mole!)

Tap **🌳 Backyard** at the top of the pool to head back. (Mansion → Backyard →
Pool — wander between all three areas freely.)

## Pets & customizing them 🐾

Buy a pet (chick, kitten, puppy, bunny or star buddy) in the Gift Shop's **Pets**
tab — it follows you around both the mansion and the backyard. Then open the
**🎨 My Pet** tab to make it your own:

- **Colour** — recolour your pet (pink, blue, mint, lavender… or keep it natural)
- **Accessory** — give it a bow, a flower or a little crown
- **Name** — type your pet's name

Your pet shows off its colour, accessory and name everywhere it follows you.

## Dressing up 👗

Tap the **Dress Up** button (top of the mansion) or walk up to the wardrobe
mirror to open a quick menu — change your hair, outfit colors, accessory and
pet right there without leaving the mansion, with a live preview. It also has
shortcuts to the full character editor and the Gift Shop.

## The Gift Shop 🎀

Walk up to Pochacco's gift shop counter in the mansion (or click it) to spend
your coins on:

- **Tops & bottoms** — extra outfit colors
- **Items** — fun headwear: party hat, headphones, flower crown, halo, wizard
  hat and a royal crown
- **Pets** — a chick, kitten, puppy, bunny or a sparkly star buddy that follows
  you around the mansion

Buying something puts it on right away, and everything you unlock shows up in
the dress-up mirror so you can mix and match. Tap an owned pet again to put it
away. Earn more coins by playing the games!
