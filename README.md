# 🎀 Cinnamoroll Mansion

A cute fan-made browser game for kids 6–12. Create your own character (girl or
boy — pick skin, hair, outfit and accessories), then explore the mansion and
play mini-games with all the Hello Kitty friends!

## How to play

Just open `index.html` in any modern browser (double-click it), or serve the
folder for best results:

```sh
python3 -m http.server 8000
# then visit http://localhost:8000
```

No installs, no build step — everything is plain HTML5 canvas + JavaScript.

### On a tablet 📱

The game is tablet-ready in any mobile browser. Hold the tablet **sideways
(landscape)** — if you're in portrait it'll show a friendly "turn me sideways"
screen. Everything scales to fit the screen, with no accidental zooming or
scrolling, and it respects rounded corners / notches. Tap the **fullscreen
button** (top right) to hide the browser bars, or "Add to Home Screen" to play
it like a real app.

## Controls

| Action | Mouse | Keyboard | Touch |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Walk | click where to go | WASD / Arrow keys | drag on the left side, or tap where to go |
| Talk / confirm | click a friend / button | Space / Enter / E | tap a friend, or the ★ button |
| Quit a mini-game | Escape | ✕ button (top right) |
| Mute / unmute | M | speaker button (top right) |
| Fullscreen | — | ⛶ button (top right, in the mansion) |

## 🗺 Cinnamon Town (the overworld)

When you press **Continue** (or finish making your character) you arrive in
**Cinnamon Town** — a cute little town map that's the hub for everything. Walk
around the plaza (with its splashy fountain) and walk up to — or just tap — any
building to visit that area:

- 🏰 **Mansion** · 🏫 **School** · ☕ **Cafe** · 🏊 **Pool** (along the top)
- 👗 **Boutique** · 🛝 **Playground** · 🌳 **Backyard** (along the bottom)

Once you're inside an area, tap the **🗺 Town** button (top of the screen) any
time to pop back out to the town and pick somewhere else. The town is the only
way between areas — there are no doors directly linking one area to another.

## The games

- 🎳 **Bowling** with Hello Kitty
- ⚾ **Baseball** with Pompompurin
- 🌼 **Hide & Seek** in the garden with Cinnamoroll
- 🎴 **Memory Match** with My Melody
- 🐟 **Fishing Pond** with Keroppi
- 🎵 **Dance Party** with Kuromi
- 🚦 **Red Light, Green Light** with Badtz-Maru — walk on green, freeze on red!
  (talk to him on the sofa)
- ⭐ **Coin Hunt** with Pochacco — run around the room grabbing goodies before
  the timer (ask him at the shop)

The last two are walk-around games you start by talking to the friends hanging
out in the mansion.

### 🌳 The Backyard

Pick the **🌳 Backyard** from the town map to reach the outdoor area, where
all the same friends are hanging out for some outdoor games:

- ⛳ **Putt-Putt** mini-golf with Pompompurin — pull back and putt!
- 🎵 **Follow the Leader** with Cinnamoroll — copy the moves!
- 🔢 **Hopscotch** with Hello Kitty — hop the squares in order
- ⚽ **Penalty Kicks** with Pochacco — aim past Keroppi the goalie
- 💦 **Water Balloon Toss** with Badtz-Maru — splash the targets
- 🪢 **Jump Rope** with Kuromi — time your jumps!
- 🚦 **Red Light, Green Light** — Keroppi points you to Badtz-Maru's game
- 🦋 **Butterfly Catch** with My Melody — run around with your net

Every friend hosts an outdoor game now! Walk up to a friend to play, then tap
**🗺 Town** to head back to the map and choose another area. Earn coins in every
game — your coins and best scores are saved automatically in your browser.

## The Pool 🏊

Pick the **🏊 Pool** from the town map to reach the swimming pool, with six
splashy water games — all the same friends are here too:

- 🌊 **Cannonball Splash** with Keroppi — time your bounce for the biggest splash
- 🏊 **Pool Race** with Pochacco — tap fast to swim and beat Badtz-Maru
- 🛝 **Water Slide** with Cinnamoroll — steer down the slide and grab the rings
- 🏐 **Beach Ball** with Pompompurin — keep the ball bouncing
- 🦆 **Duck Round-Up** with My Melody — tap the rubber duckies before they drift off
- 🔫 **Squirt Splash** with Kuromi — squirt the targets that pop up (whack-a-mole!)

Tap **🗺 Town** to head back to the map.

## The Playground 🛝

Pick the **🛝 Playground** from the town map to reach the playground, with six
more games — the same friends are here too:

- 🥏 **Swing Jump** with My Melody — pump the swing, then jump off and fly far
- 🏃 **Tag, You're It!** with Pochacco — chase and tag all your friends
- 🐒 **Monkey Bars** with Badtz-Maru — time your taps to swing across
- 🏰 **Sand Castle** with Hello Kitty — stack the blocks to build it tall
- 🪁 **Kite Flying** with Cinnamoroll — steer the kite through rings and gusts
- ⚖️ **Seesaw Pop** with Pompompurin — bounce Keroppi up to pop the balloons

Tap **🗺 Town** to head back to the map.

## The School 🏫

Pick the **🏫 School** from the town map to reach the schoolyard, with a ride
there plus learning games for a kindergartener heading into 1st grade — the
same friends are the teachers:

- 🏍️ **Ride to School** with Badtz-Maru — jump your motorbike over obstacles
- 🔢 **Counting Fun** with My Melody — count the objects, tap the number
- ➕ **Add It Up** with Pompompurin — simple adding (sums to ~10)
- 🔤 **ABC Match** with Hello Kitty — letters and beginning sounds
- ✏️ **Spell It Out** with Cinnamoroll — finish the little (CVC) word
- 🔷 **Shapes & Patterns** with Keroppi — what shape comes next?

## The Cafe ☕

Pick the **☕ Cafe** from the town map to reach the Cinnamoroll Cafe, where the
friends are the chefs and you play *Cooking-Mama-style* cooking games — quick,
hands-on kitchen tasks:

- 🔪 **Chop Chop!** with Keroppi — tap to bring the knife down on the glowing line
- 🥣 **Mix It Up** with My Melody — hold to stir the batter until it's fluffy
- 🥞 **Flip 'Em!** with Pompompurin — flip each pancake when it turns golden
- 🫖 **Pour It!** with Hello Kitty — hold to pour, let go right at the fill line
- 🧁 **Frost the Cupcakes** with Cinnamoroll — match the frosting and topping to the order
- 🥪 **Make a Sandwich** with Pochacco — stack the ingredients in the right order

Nothing burns and nobody loses — a slightly dark pancake or a little spill is
just funny. Tap **🗺 Town** to head back to the map.

## The Boutique 👗

Pick the **👗 Boutique** from the town map to reach the Boutique, where you
**make your own clothes!** Walk up to **My Melody's Design Studio** to open the
designer and create a custom outfit:

- **Garment** — a top, a skirt, or a whole dress
- **Colour** — pick from a rainbow of fabric colours
- **Pattern** — solid, stripes, polka dots, hearts, stars, checks or rainbow
- **Trim** — a contrasting colour for the collar and hem
- **Motif** — add a little heart, star, flower, bow or dot on the front

Your design updates live on your character. Tap **👗 Wear It!** to put it on
(it shows everywhere you go), or **💾 Save** it to your **Closet** to wear again
later. Open **🗂 My Closet** (or the My Designs button) to re-wear or remove any
saved design. There's also a **🪞 mirror** for the regular Dress-Up menu.

All seven areas hang off the **🗺 Cinnamon Town** overworld — pick any of them
from the town map, and hop back to the town from any area with the **🗺 Town**
button. The town is the hub: areas no longer link directly to one another, so
every trip between areas goes through the town map.

## Pets & customizing them 🐾

Buy a pet (chick, kitten, puppy, bunny or star buddy) in the Gift Shop's **Pets**
tab — it follows you around both the mansion and the backyard. Then open the
**🎨 My Pet** tab to make it your own:

- **Colour** — recolour your pet (pink, blue, mint, lavender… or keep it natural)
- **Accessory** — give it a bow, a flower or a little crown
- **Name** — type your pet's name

Your pet shows off its colour, accessory and name everywhere it follows you.

## Dressing up 👗

Tap the **Dress Up** button (top of the mansion) or walk up to the wardrobe
mirror to open a quick menu — change your hair, outfit colors, accessory and
pet right there without leaving the mansion, with a live preview. It also has
shortcuts to the full character editor and the Gift Shop.

## The Gift Shop 🎀

Walk up to Pochacco's gift shop counter in the mansion (or click it) to spend
your coins on:

- **Tops & bottoms** — extra outfit colors
- **Items** — fun headwear: party hat, headphones, flower crown, halo, wizard
  hat and a royal crown
- **Pets** — a chick, kitten, puppy, bunny or a sparkly star buddy that follows
  you around the mansion

Buying something puts it on right away, and everything you unlock shows up in
the dress-up mirror so you can mix and match. Tap an owned pet again to put it
away. Earn more coins by playing the games!
